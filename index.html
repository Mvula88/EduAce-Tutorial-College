About Us

Your Path to Academic Excellence

EduAce Tutorial College is a registered examination and tuition centre dedicated to helping Grade 11 and 12 part-time students achieve their academic goals.

We offer tutorials to students who wish to improve their results and first-time Grade 11 learners. Our mission is simple: "We have the patience and believe that every learner is somebody."
